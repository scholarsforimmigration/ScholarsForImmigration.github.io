Our Mission and Vision

Scholars for Immigration's mission is to provide free ESL-focused tutoring and support to immigrants and those who are a part of underserved populations.

Scholars for Immigration's vision is to provide a judgment-free zone with tutoring catered towards ESL students and opportunities in all sorts of professional fields.

What We Do

ESL Tutoring Program

We connect English-learning, immigrant or ESL-program students to tutors to help with homework, the language learning process and work on a 1-on-1 mentorship level, free of charge.

Panels and Workshops

We hold professional development, guidance and networking events to provide insight into relevant and complex circumstances surrounding immigration, connect those of immigrant backgrounds, and give pressing career and academic advise to marginalized communities.

Fellowship

We deliver professional, scholarship and academic opportunities, ensure access to workshops and events and networking opportunities to our fellows through our unique and flexible fellowship program developed to strengthen ties in the immigrant community and close the achievement gap.
